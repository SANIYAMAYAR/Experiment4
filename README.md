# Experiment4

Aim
A small program hides within Android gadgets, made just to combine a pair of numbers. Numbers go into spaces seen toward the top part of the display. Pressing Add makes the machine grab both inputs, join them, place total below. Hitting Reset clears every digit entered, result wiped out completely. From that point forward, fresh calculations begin again without clutter.

Fingers meet glass, where symbols rise from typed lines. A field grabs keystrokes, another listens for touches, a third fills with replies. Pressing triggers Java scripts that split inputs apart. Text turns to numbers, losing their look-as-written shape. Values move through layers below, each step changing how they appear after. A finger meets the surface, pulling broken pieces into alignment below. When pressure hits the active zone, things begin to move. That is where the change begins.


Algorithm
Start the application.
A small screen appears across the Android device, shaped like a short questionnaire. One box follows another, each waiting for typed input. Alongside, two controls lie flat, inactive yet ready. Below, blank space stretches, saved for answers once they show up. Everything links without extra pieces, clean and fitted together.
Start by typing a single number in the first box that prompts you. Place just one numeral where the field appears initially.
Below, type the second number. Same as earlier, waiting there quietly till you press something.

Every time someone presses Add, pull data from the top two spots right away. The moment that button gets pressed, grab all numbers sitting in either field. Hitting plus means pulling content directly from whatever boxes are showing. Once the add feature runs, it takes whatever was typed into one or both places. Press the total option and it pulls together all characters entered in both sections.
Look inside each box to see if there are words. If they hold nothing, just notice how they sit quiet and bare.
Once each entry appears accurate, digits take the place of terms. Then everything shifts without warning. Words vanish where numbers now stand. Only numerals remain in positions once held by letters. The moment clarity arrives, transformation begins again.
A shape begins with one line, then another slips beside. Progress happens when they meet through slow layers. Each movement pulls the pieces along. Finally, pair turns whole by quiet growth.

A shape shows up on screen after calculations complete. Once arithmetic ends, different numbers take its place.
When nothing shows up inside those boxes, show a note that asks for real numbers instead. Sometimes blanks mean someone forgot to type digits - point it out gently. A quiet reminder works better than silence. Numbers belong here, not empty space. Let the user know without shouting. Missing info? Just say so plainly. Ask once. Wait.
Right after hitting Reset, all boxes turn empty. The moment it activates, whatever was typed disappears entirely. Pushing that button clears each field without delay. Letters or words - vanished instantly, leaving bare spaces behind. Click once, and both sides lose everything straight away.

A bit ahead, wipe away whatever shows up on the display.
Each try needs different numbers typed. The moment you hit enter, everything wipes clean. Once the screen goes blank, start over again. Submitting your input moves all entries forward. New figures trigger another chain of steps. Every time figures shift, begin again from zero. Hit return to wipe the slate clean.

After leaving the app, the active task shuts down. When the user closes it, operations inside halt immediately. Exiting means everything running pauses right then. The moment they step out, ongoing processes cease entirely. Once outside the interface, whatever was working stops dead.
